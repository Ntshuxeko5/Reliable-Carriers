## Getting a Quote

### Option 1: Standard Package Quote

1. Click **"Get Quote"** on the homepage
2. Enter package details:
   - **Pickup Address** (type or select from map)
   - **Delivery Address** (type or select from map)
   - **Package Weight** (in kg)
   - **Package Dimensions** (length, width, height in cm)
   - **Package Type** (document, parcel, fragile, etc.)
   - **Urgency** (standard, express, same-day)
3. Click **"Calculate Quote"**
4. Review the estimated price and delivery time
5. Click **"Proceed to Book"**

### Option 2: Furniture Moving Quote

1. Go to **"Furniture Moving"** page
2. Fill in moving details:
   - **Pickup & Delivery Addresses**
   - **Items to Move** (sofa, bed, etc.)
   - **Apartment Floor** (if applicable)
   - **Special Requirements** (disassembly, wrapping, etc.)
3. Get your quote and proceed to booking

### Understanding Your Quote

- **Base Price**: Distance and weight calculation
- **Service Type**: Standard, Express, or Same-Day
- **Special Handling**: Fragile items, insurance, etc.
- **Total Cost**: All fees included

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I track my package?
**A:** Use your tracking number on the Track Package page or view from your dashboard.

### Q: Can I cancel a booking?
**A:** Yes, if package hasn't been collected. Contact support or cancel from dashboard.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** Credit/debit cards, bank transfer, and mobile money via Paystack.

### Q: How long does delivery take?
**A:** Standard: 1-3 business days, Express: Same day, Same-Day: Within hours.

### Q: Do you deliver on weekends?
**A:** Yes, with Express and Same-Day services. Standard delivery is business days only.

### Q: What if my package is damaged?
**A:** Contact support immediately. If insured, file a claim. We investigate all cases.

### Q: Can I reschedule delivery?
**A:** Yes, contact support or use your dashboard if package hasn't been delivered yet.

### Q: How do I get a refund?
**A:** Contact support with your tracking number. Refunds processed within 5-7 business days.
