Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my package?

You can track your package using the tracking number provided when you booked your service. Simply enter the tracking number on our tracking page for real-time updates.

What are your delivery times?

We offer various delivery options including same-day, overnight, and economy delivery. Delivery times depend on the service type and location.

Do you provide moving services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive moving services including furniture moving, load transport, and complete house moving services nationwide.

How do I book a service?

You can book a service through our online booking system, by calling our customer service, or by visiting our office. Online booking is available 24/7.

What areas do you serve?

Our courier services are currently available in Gauteng Province, while our moving services are available nationwide across South Africa.

Do you provide insurance?

Yes, all our services include basic insurance coverage. Additional insurance options are available for high-value items and special requirements.
