Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

You can track your package in several ways:

  • Use our online tracking tool at /tracking
  • Enter your tracking number on our website
  • Call our customer service at 0800-RELIABLE
  • Check your email for tracking updates

Our delivery times vary by service type:

  • Express Delivery: Same-day or next-day
  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days
  • Economy Delivery: 4-7 business days
  • International: 5-14 business days

You can calculate shipping costs using our online quote tool:

  1. Go to our quote page
  2. Enter pickup and delivery addresses
  3. Specify package dimensions and weight
  4. Select service type
  5. Get instant pricing

We take package security seriously:

  • All packages are insured up to R5,000
  • Report damage within 48 hours of delivery
  • We'll investigate and process claims quickly
  • Contact our claims department at claims@reliablecarriers.co.za

Yes, you can schedule pickups in several ways:

  • Use our online booking system
  • Call our customer service
  • Use our mobile app
  • Schedule recurring pickups for business customers

We accept various payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards
  • Bank transfers
  • PayPal
  • Cash on delivery (selected areas)
  • Monthly billing for business customers
