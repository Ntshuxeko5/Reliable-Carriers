Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?

If you exceed your monthly shipment limit, additional shipments will be charged at our standard rates. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit.

Do you offer custom pricing for large volumes?

Yes, we offer custom pricing for businesses with high shipping volumes. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and get a tailored quote.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. You only pay the monthly subscription fee and shipping costs.
