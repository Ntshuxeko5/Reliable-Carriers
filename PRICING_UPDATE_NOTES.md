# Pricing Update - Realistic South African Rates

## Overview
Updated pricing to reflect realistic South African courier market rates based on research of major courier services (PostNet, Fastway, CourierGuy, etc.) as of 2025.

## Changes Made

### Base Prices (for first 15km, up to 5kg)
- **Economy**: R120 (was R450) - 73% reduction
- **Standard**: R180 (was R550) - 67% reduction  
- **Express**: R350 (was R750) - 53% reduction
- **Same-Day**: R550 (was R950) - 42% reduction

### Distance Charges (per km after 15km)
- **Economy**: R8.50/km (was R15/km)
- **Standard**: R12/km (was R25/km)
- **Express**: R20/km (was R35/km)
- **Same-Day**: R30/km (was R50/km)

### Weight Charges
- **Base Weight**: 5kg included (was 1kg)
- **Additional Weight**: R8/kg after 5kg (was R5/kg after 1kg)
- More realistic for typical package weights

### Fees
- **Fuel Surcharge**: 8% (was 5%) - reflects higher fuel costs in SA
- **Service Fee**: 5% (was 3%) - platform/processing fee
- **Insurance**: 2.5% (was 2%) - optional coverage

### Base Distance
- **Free Distance**: 15km (was 20km) - more realistic for urban deliveries
